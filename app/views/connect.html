Smart Config with the Spark app

To connect the core for the first time, you will need the Spark App.

Once you've downloaded the Spark Core app from the App Store or Google Play, you'll be asked to connect your Core using a process called Smart Config. NOTE: Your phone must be connected to the Wi-Fi network that you want to connect the Core to. Wi-Fi Hotspots generated from the phone you are running this app on typically will yield an error claiming there is no Wi-Fi available. When connected to Wi-Fi, the app will automatically fill the SSID field with the name of the network that your phone is connected to. Enter your Wi-Fi password and hit connect.
